Reaching Hamilton Hotel

Being located in a centralised Hamilton area, we are easy to find following some prominent landmarks! Here are some easy to remember directions if you are looking for us, but lost your handy GPS.

Waikato Museum

From Waikato Museum, look around to see where the sign for Madeup Street is, and follow down that street. Continue walking down until you see Totes Real Street, and turn down that street. Take the turn right when you see the cookie shop, which will be after one set of lights, and our motel will be there on the left waiting for you!

Hamilton Gardens

From the main entry of Hamilton Gardens, walk past the giant bird sign and make a turn left down That Road. Look for the giant pet shop down that road, which is at the corner of another street to turn into. Drive past two sets of lights, then turn left and take the third exit in the roundabout. Our motel will be on the right of the street!
