Rooms

Here at Hamilton Hotel we provide 3 different types of rooms of your choosing that you can stay in. All rooms are provided with a television with basic channels, free Wi-Fi for up to 4 devices, a small kitchen with a microwave and fridge and a bathroom with free toiletries. Breakfast is complimentary with each room type.



Studio

Our studio rooms are compact, cozy, and great value.

  • Rooms up to two people.
  • One main room and one bathroom.
  • No couch.
  • Main room has living area, kitchen and bed.
  • Basic bathroom with shower.
  • A bunk bed.
A studio room costs $200 a night.


One Bedroom Unit

Our one bedroom units provide more space to relax than our studios.

  • Rooms up to three people, four if one wants to sleep on the couch.
  • One bedroom, a living area, a kitchen and a bathroom.
  • Kitchen has an oven.
  • Bathroom has a bath as well as a shower.
  • A double bed. Can be made into single beds. An extra bed can be provided.
A one bedroom unit costs $300 a night.


Two Bedroom Unit

Our two bedroom units provide all the comfort of our single bedroom units, but with another bedroom to allow more to stay.

  • Rooms up to five people, six if one wants to sleep on the couch.
  • One bedroom, a living area, a kitchen and a bathroom.
  • Kitchen has an oven.
  • Bathroom has a bath as well as a shower.
  • Two double beds, one in each room. Beds can be made into single beds, and an extra bed can be provided.
A two bedroom unit costs $400 a night.
